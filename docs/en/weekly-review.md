# Weekly review

[Docs index](../README.md) · [Español](../es/weekly-review.md)

The weekly review helps you understand how your week actually went, not just how busy it felt.

## Quick path

1. Open **Weekly review**
2. Review area and quest performance
3. Read the generated insights
4. Write your reflection
5. Save the review

## What the review includes

- XP total
- completed quests
- failed quests
- success rate
- area performance
- quest performance
- reflection fields
- insights

## Reflection fields

LifeQuest stores a structured review with:

- what went well
- what to improve
- next intention
- mood/state

## Insights

Insights can point to things like:

- your best day
- a declining area
- a strong area
- a streak milestone
- an abandoned quest

## How to use it well

- do it on the same day every week
- look for patterns, not perfect performance
- adjust quests that repeatedly fail
- lower friction before raising ambition
