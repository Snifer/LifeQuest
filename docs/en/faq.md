# FAQ

[Docs index](../README.md) · [Español](../es/faq.md)

## Does LifeQuest work locally?

Yes. LifeQuest is local-first and stores data inside your vault.

## Does it use the internet?

Only the optional remote daily message source uses the network when you configure it.

## Where is my data stored?

- `_LifeQuest/data.json`
- `_LifeQuest/health/historial.csv` for health CSV exports

## Does it work on mobile?

The plugin is work on mobile, but your experience can vary depending on your workflow and setup.

## Can I change the language later?

Yes. You can change it from settings.

## Can I disable add-ons?

Yes. Shop and health are optional.

## Can I reset the plugin?

Yes. There is a factory reset option in settings. Export your data first if you want a backup.
